# Business Impact Report — EPF Calculator

> Estimates below are directional planning figures based on the Indian search
> landscape for salaried-finance queries. Actual volumes/difficulty vary and shift
> seasonally; validate against Search Console + a keyword tool post-launch. Nothing
> here is a guarantee of ranking or traffic.

## Snapshot

| Metric                            | Estimate                                                                                                                                                                             |
| --------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Primary keyword**               | epf calculator                                                                                                                                                                       |
| **Monthly search volume (India)** | ~150,000–250,000 for the head terms ("epf calculator" + "pf calculator"); ~500,000+ across the full cluster (provident fund, epf interest, employee provident fund, eps, long-tails) |
| **Keyword difficulty**            | Medium–High (~45–60) for head terms; Low–Medium (~15–35) for how-to and long-tail                                                                                                    |
| **Search intent**                 | Predominantly transactional/tool + informational (contribution split, interest, tax)                                                                                                 |
| **Expected CTR**                  | ~6–12% at positions 3–6; ~20–30% at positions 1–2 with an FAQ rich result                                                                                                            |
| **Seasonality**                   | Peaks when EPFO announces the annual rate, and around appraisal/ITR season                                                                                                           |

## Top competitors

- **Groww** — high authority, ranks broadly for PF/retirement calculators.
- **ClearTax / ET Money** — strong brand and content depth.
- **BankBazaar / Paisabazaar** — broad calculator coverage, ad-heavy.
- **EPFO portal/passbook** — authoritative but backward-looking (history, not projection).
- **Scripbox / Cleartax blogs** — content on EPF rules and comparisons.

**Where we win:** faster, private (in-browser), no signup/ads; explicit EPS split
(8.33% capped at ₹15,000); EPFO monthly running-balance interest method; year-wise
projection with a growth chart; editable rate/increment; CSV/share; visible official
sources (EPFO, EPF Act, Ministry of Labour).

## Expected internal links

- **Inbound to the calculator:** Income Tax + HRA (relatedTools already form the tax
  hub), homepage (featured), /popular, /new, sitemap, and all three supporting
  articles (primary CTA in each) → 6+ internal entry points at launch.
- **Outbound from the calculator:** income-tax, hra, ppf, sip, fd, home-loan
  (relatedTools) — routing users deeper into the salary → retirement ecosystem.
- **Article cross-links:** 3 articles interlink and each links the calculator + PPF +
  SIP + Income Tax (+ HRA where relevant).

## Estimated organic opportunity

- **Cluster ceiling:** given head-term volume and long-tail breadth, the EPF topic can
  realistically drive **tens of thousands of organic sessions/month** at maturity,
  concentrated around rate-announcement and appraisal seasons.
- **High tool engagement:** strong transactional intent means a large share of clicks
  will use the calculator, lifting dwell time and internal navigation to Income Tax,
  HRA, PPF, and SIP — compounding authority across both the tax and retirement hubs.
- **Cluster compounding:** cross-linking with the shipped Income Tax and HRA
  calculators strengthens the whole salary/retirement hub, lifting sibling pages.

## Why this calculator is strategically important

1. **Opens the retirement cluster.** Income Tax and HRA built the salary/tax hub; EPF
   is the first retirement spoke, extending topical authority toward PPF/NPS/retirement
   — exactly the cluster-expansion objective in the brief.
2. **Bridges salary → EPF → EPS → retirement.** EPF naturally connects wages (HRA,
   Income Tax) to long-term corpus (PPF, SIP), seeding future NPS/gratuity calculators
   with a high-traffic, interlinked anchor.
3. **Very high, recurring demand.** EPF is among the highest-volume salaried-finance
   queries, searched every rate announcement and appraisal cycle — durable traffic.
4. **Low marginal cost.** Built entirely on the frozen Tool Framework with no new
   shared components or engine changes — pure additive SEO leverage.
5. **Defensible differentiation.** The explicit EPS split, EPFO interest method, and
   year-wise growth projection address the exact confusions competitors gloss over.
