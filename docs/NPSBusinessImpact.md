# Business Impact Report — NPS Calculator

> Estimates below are directional planning figures based on the Indian search
> landscape for retirement-finance queries. Actual volumes/difficulty vary and shift
> seasonally; validate against Search Console + a keyword tool post-launch. Nothing
> here is a guarantee of ranking or traffic.

## Snapshot

| Metric                            | Estimate                                                                                                                                                      |
| --------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Primary keyword**               | nps calculator                                                                                                                                                |
| **Monthly search volume (India)** | ~110,000–200,000 for the head term; ~350,000+ across the full cluster (nps maturity, corpus, pension, tax benefit, 80ccd, national pension system long-tails) |
| **Keyword difficulty**            | Medium–High (~45–60) for the head term; Low–Medium (~15–35) for how-to, tax, and long-tail                                                                    |
| **Search intent**                 | Predominantly transactional/tool + informational (tax benefits, pension, withdrawal)                                                                          |
| **Expected CTR**                  | ~6–12% at positions 3–6; ~20–30% at positions 1–2 with an FAQ rich result                                                                                     |
| **Seasonality**                   | Peaks in Dec–Feb (investment declarations) and Jun–Jul (ITR filing)                                                                                           |

## Top competitors

- **Groww** — high authority, ranks broadly for retirement calculators.
- **ClearTax / ET Money** — strong brand and content depth.
- **NPS Trust / PFRDA calculator** — authoritative but dated UX.
- **BankBazaar / Paisabazaar** — broad calculator coverage, ad-heavy.
- **Scripbox / Cleartax blogs** — content on NPS rules and tax.

**Where we win:** faster, private (in-browser), no signup/ads; adjustable lump-sum/
annuity split (0–60%); monthly pension estimate; year-wise corpus growth chart;
explicit tax benefits (80CCD(1)/(1B)/(2)); CSV/share; visible official sources
(PFRDA, NPS Trust, Income Tax Department).

## Estimated organic opportunity

- **Cluster ceiling:** given head-term volume and long-tail breadth, the NPS topic can
  realistically drive **tens of thousands of organic sessions/month** at maturity,
  concentrated around the two tax-season peaks.
- **High tool engagement:** strong transactional intent means a large share of clicks
  will use the calculator, lifting dwell time and internal navigation to EPF, PPF,
  Gratuity, Income Tax, and SIP — compounding authority across the whole hub.
- **Cluster completion:** as the final retirement spoke, NPS closes the salary →
  retirement journey, strengthening topical authority for every sibling page.

## Why this calculator is strategically important

1. **Completes the retirement cluster.** Income Tax + HRA + EPF + Gratuity + PPF + SIP
   built the salary/retirement hub; NPS is the capstone, signalling comprehensive
   coverage of salaried retirement planning — the exact objective in the brief.
2. **Closes the salary → retirement journey.** NPS ties earning, saving, and exit
   benefits into a single lifelong-pension outcome, reinforcing links across the cluster.
3. **High, tax-driven demand.** NPS is searched heavily every declaration and filing
   season, largely for its unique ₹50,000 (80CCD(1B)) deduction — durable, recurring traffic.
4. **Low marginal cost.** Built entirely on the frozen Tool Framework with no new
   shared components or engine changes — pure additive SEO leverage.
5. **Defensible differentiation.** The adjustable lump-sum split, pension estimate,
   growth projection, and tax-benefit explainer address the exact confusions
   competitors gloss over.
